As part of the MoMA acquisition, we have developed an interactive visualization to pair with the physical installation of our piece. This interactive opens up new ways of filtering and understanding the data, brings the anthromes data up to the year 2025, and includes multiple ways of creating links and overlaps between the two datasets.
